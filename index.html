Why it matters

A waiting line is the first impression your service makes.

When visitors don't know how long they'll wait, and staff can't see what's coming next, everyone loses time. Yes Queue gives both sides of the counter the information they need.

Visitors wait blind

Without a ticket system, people don't know their position in line or how long it will take — so they crowd the counter and ask repeatedly.

Staff shout names across the room

Calling names or numbers out loud doesn't scale past a handful of people, and it's the first thing visitors complain about.

Managers fly blind

Without real numbers, it's guesswork whether a queue needs another counter open, or which agent needs support during peak hours.

Security & reliability

Built with data isolation as a first principle.

Every client's data is kept apart by design — not just by convention.

Database-level tenant isolation

Every query is restricted to your own organization's data at the database layer itself, as a second line of defense beyond application-level checks.

Industry-standard password hashing

Every password is hashed with Argon2 before it's ever stored — plain-text passwords are never written to disk.

Permanent audit trail

Every meaningful administrative action — who changed what, and when — is recorded and cannot be edited or deleted from the Admin Panel.

Role-based access control

What each staff member can see and do is governed by their assigned role, checked on every single request.
